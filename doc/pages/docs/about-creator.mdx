# About Creator

Know about creator of &zwnj;<AwesomeLoaders/>

<Profile profileUrl={ProfileImage} />

I am [**Ashutosh Hathidara**](http://ashutoshhathidara.com/), Artificial Intelligence Engineer at Legato Health Technology. Professionally, I work as AI developer, but I have keen interest as well as experience in working with `Full Stack Development`, `UI/UX Design` and `Deep Learning`. I love curating animations for web using `ReactJS` and `Gatsby`.

Working for open source community has been my passion for last several years. To satisfy myself, I have created several open source projects like [MasterPortfolio](https://github.com/ashutosh1919/masterPortfolio), [React LiquidSwipe](https://github.com/ashutosh1919/react-liquidswipe), [Truvisory](https://github.com/ashutosh1919/truvisory) and many more. Among these, &zwnj;<AwesomeLoaders/> has been the most awaited and exciting project. I have made valuable contributions to some of the most impactful projects like [Tensorflow](https://github.com/tensorflow/tensorflow), [Scikit-learn](https://github.com/scikit-learn/scikit-learn) etc. All raised PRs and Issues can be found [here](https://ashutoshhathidara.com/#/opensource).

I own a [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/c/DevSense19) where I create awesome video tutorials related to `Web`, `UI/UX Design` and `Machine Learning`.
